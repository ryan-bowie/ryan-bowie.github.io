About Me

I am a photographer and journalist of all trades. I have photographed sports such as lacrosse, gymnastics and basketball while also taking on assignments for general news as well. Outside of being a photographer, I am a proud Army veteran. My passion for photography came after photographing a double rainbow that appeared following the first storm in over two months in the dry and sandy country of Syria. Seeing something so beautiful in the darkest of times sparked my inspiration. I bought a Nikon D7500 after returning to the U.S. and the rest is history that is too long for this about page.
