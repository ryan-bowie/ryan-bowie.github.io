Baked Croissant French Toast

A more intricate spin on an average everyday breakfast favorite, using store bought croissants instead of the standard sandwich or brioche bread normally used for french toast.

This recipe is one that I keep in my personal recipe collection and I like to break it out when making breakfast for a large group of people or just on a weekend where I want something a little more special to start my day.

Serves: 3-5 people

Prep Time: 20 min

Cook Time: 45 min

Ingredients

    Custard

  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • pinch of nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups milk, almond or dairy

    • Bread

  • 8-10 croissants

    • Caramelized Base

  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar

    • Garnish

  • 1 cup strawberries and blueberries
  • whipped cream, either homemade or store bought
  • dusting of Confectioner's sugar

Instructions

  1. Whisk together ingredients for custard until combined, ADD MILK LAST.
  2. Add butter to small saucepan over medium heat until fully melted. Add brown sugar and whisk until fully combined.
  3. Slice croissants lengthwise and spread caramelized base in a greased 9x12 casserole dish
  4. Organize croissants in unifrom way and pour custard over.
  5. Cover with foil and let it sit for 25 min, or overnight in fridge.
  6. Bake at 350Â°F w/ foil for 30 min.
  7. Remove foil and bake for another 15 min.
  8. Let cool for 10 min, garnish with powdered sugar, berries and whipped cream.
